Did You Know Every Episode Intro Had Different Music?


What sounds were used to make the theme song?

Jonathan Wolff recorded samples of lip pops, tongue noises and other “organic human sounds” to create what he described as a “percolating rhythm, this New York groove” to play around Seinfeld’s dialogue
